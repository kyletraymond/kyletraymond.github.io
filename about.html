Welcome to Toadstools!

My name is Kyle Raymond. I am an aspiring engineer, writer, and artist. Toadstools is my mushroom patch: poems, graphic poems, Christmas card art - a blog post about science or mathematics or the last novel I read - whatever I create that manages to fruit and spore might find its way here.

I also love terrible metaphors. My mind is, indeed, the rotting forest floor from which these mushrooms bloom. Or is it a fungus, creeping though detris, transforming decay into art?

You get the idea. I have an instagram account full of fun, false-start art. The code for this site was generated with Jekyll and is hosted on github.
